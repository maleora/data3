More About Tomorrow and Beyond: Masterpieces of Science Fiction Art

A fun overview of science fiction book covers of the 1960's and 70's. There's only a brief section of text at the beginning, followed by page after page of color artwork from many of the prominent illustrators of the time. Funky, psychedelic, disco-era science fiction book cover art. This book is an interesting period piece of the sort of illustration that was current in the early and mid-1970s. To be fair, there is plenty that would not look out of place today. But those Peter-Max-drug-trippy images do linger in the memory.
