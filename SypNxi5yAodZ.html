More About Ìsarà: A Voyage Around "Essay"

Reading this book was certainly a different experience. I've read several by Brits and others about life in colonial Africa from their perspective. This is my first written by an African. Perhaps not so oddly, it reminds me of stories of colonial India. Nice reading A well written account of childhood in pre independence Yorubaland. Although it is probably not typical, it illustrates the essence of the Yoruba parenting style, a style that makes every adult a parent and every child dutiful. I recommend it.
